How It Works

Four Parts. One Watchful System.

From the soil to your pocket — here's how SWMS keeps an eye on your farm.

1

Moisture Transmitter In your soil

"A small device in your soil. Like having eyes underground, 24/7."

Measures soil moisture, temperature, and humidity — and reports in every 10 minutes, rain or shine.

2

Central Processing Unit The brain

"The brain. Checks weather, checks soil, decides what's best — never gets tired."

Combines your soil readings with the weather forecast and decides: water now, or wait.

3

Valve Control System Optional — for irrigation users

"Waters automatically and detects leaks instantly."

Opens and closes the valve, measures water flow, and catches leaks or clogs. Non-irrigation farmers skip this — you still get all the data!

4

Mobile App In your pocket

"Your farm in your pocket. See everything, control everything."

Dashboard, instant notifications, weekly reports, farm setup, and device status — all in one place.

Why GreenGrid Tech Exists

I started GreenGrid Tech because I believe agriculture in Africa deserves better technology. Too often, farming is seen as outdated and difficult — despite being one of the most important sectors on the continent.

Through conversations with farmers, I saw recurring challenges: water loss, crop loss, unnecessary transport costs, and limited visibility into farm conditions. SWMS was created to address these realities and help farmers make better decisions with confidence.

My vision is simple: make farming smarter, more attractive, and more profitable through practical technology.
